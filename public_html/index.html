About me

Since October 2020, I am a PhD student at the University of Regensburg under the supervision of Denis-Charles Cisinski and member of the SFB 1085 - Higher Invariants funded by the DFG.
Broadly speaking I am interested in homotopy theory and its applications to algebraic geometry.
