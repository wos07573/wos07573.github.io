About me

I am a Postdoc at the University of Regensburg in the group of Denis-Charles Cisinski and member of the SFB 1085 - Higher Invariants funded by the DFG.
I completed my PhD in Dec 2024 under the supervision of Denis-Charles Cisinski. Broadly speaking I am interested in homotopy theory and its applications to algebraic geometry.
